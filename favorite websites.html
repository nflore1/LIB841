My Favorite Resources

Helpful Websites

I recommend exploring the following websites.

Teachers of Multilingual Learners

Colorin Colorado

This website provides resources and teaching strategies for supporting English language acquisition and cultural acclimation.

ELL 2.0

ELL 2.0 is a website full of strategies for English Language Learners, specifically focusing on ways to integrate technology into learning.

WIDA Can Do Descriptors

The WIDA Can Do Descriptors let teachers know what students are able to do at each level of English acquisition and can help teachers plan learning targets at appropriate levels.

Online Books

Unite for Literacy

Unite for Literacy is a collection of books with audio and translations in various languages to support multilingual literacy.

Bloom Library

Bloom Library holds an extensive collection of online books available in multiple languages, including languages that are not commonly represented.

Epic!

Epic holds a library of high-interest, ELL-friendly leveled books and audiobooks.

Community Resources

Louisville Free Public Library

Anyone in the Louisville community has access to ebooks, audiobooks, tutoring assistance, community events, and various library locations.

La Casita Center

La Casita Center is a grassroots nonprofit accompanying families in the Latinx community to empower families and create systemic change.

Americana Community Center

Americana supports the immigrant and refugee community through education, family support, youth achievement, and career and financial development.
